Privacy Policy
Last updated: January 2026
Friendly Tennis (“the App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and how it is protected.


1. Information We Collect
Personal Information
The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data such as name, email address, phone number, or identifiers.
Health Data
If you grant permission, the App may access health-related data via Apple HealthKit, such as:
Heart rate
Calories burned
Steps
Distance
Workout duration
Health data is used solely to display match statistics within the App.
This data:
is processed locally on your device
is not transmitted to any server
is not shared with third parties


2. How We Use Your Information
The App uses data only to:
display tennis match results
show optional health statistics related to a match
synchronize match results between your Apple Watch and iPhone
No data is used for advertising, analytics, or tracking.


3. Data Storage
Match results and settings are stored locally on your device
Synchronization between Apple Watch and iPhone uses Apple Watch Connectivity
No external servers or cloud storage are used


4. Subscriptions and Payments
The App offers optional subscriptions handled entirely by Apple App Store.
Payments are processed by Apple
The App does not receive or store payment information
Subscription management is handled through your Apple ID


5. Third-Party Services
The App does not use:
third-party analytics services
advertising SDKs
tracking technologies
All functionality relies on Apple system frameworks only.


6. Your Choices
You can enable or disable HealthKit access at any time in iOS Settings
You can delete the App to remove all locally stored data
You can manage subscriptions through your Apple ID settings


7. Children’s Privacy
The App does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13.


8. Changes to This Privacy Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.
Any changes will be reflected on this page.
